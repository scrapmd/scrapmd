Case 1: Authorized website

1. Tab + button on right of master view.
2. Fill https://ngs.io/2019/02/08/go-release-action/
3. Tap "Fetch ..." button
4. Tap "Save" button
5. You may see the downloaded web content stored in your local.

Case 2: Unauthorized website

1. Repeat Case 1 with URL hosted on other than ngs.io domain
2. You will see error message with "This site is not supported"
